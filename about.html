About HealthReach

HealthReach is a dedicated platform built to make healthcare accessible for everyone across Assam. Our goal is to connect patients with trusted hospitals and experienced healthcare professionals, making it easier to find the right care at the right time.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and families by providing easy access to verified healthcare information, facilitating better connections between patients and healthcare providers across Assam.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive directory of hospitals and clinics with up-to-date details
  • Detailed doctor profiles, including specialties, experience, and consultation hours
  • Location-based search to find nearby hospitals and specialists quickly
  • Interactive maps for seamless navigation to healthcare centers
  • Mobile-friendly, user-friendly platform accessible on any device
  • Continuous updates to maintain reliable and trusted healthcare information

Coverage Areas

We currently cover major healthcare hubs across Assam:

  • Tezpur
  • Guwahati
  • Dibrugarh
  • And expanding further across the state

Our Vision

To bridge the gap between patients and healthcare providers, ensuring that every individual, regardless of location or background, has access to quality medical care and support.

Data Sources & Trust

All information listed on HealthReach is carefully curated from official hospital websites, government directories, and verified medical sources. We are committed to providing accurate, up-to-date, and reliable healthcare data you can trust.

About This Project

HealthReach is a student-driven project developed at Tezpur University as part of a commitment to solving real-world challenges using technology. We aim to make a positive social impact by improving healthcare access across Assam.

Future Goals

  • Enable online appointment booking and teleconsultation services
  • Introduce patient reviews and hospital ratings
  • Expand to cover more districts and specialty healthcare centers
  • Provide health tips, awareness programs, and local healthcare news
